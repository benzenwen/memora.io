Welcome to Memora.io

Memora is (currently) an iOS and Apple Watch app that helps students memorize facts, vocabulary, terminology, or anything that can be captured in images and text. The Memora app analyzes your retention of a list of facts through the Apple Watch. Because the watch is quickly glance-able, the app can identify the optimal interval to refresh your memory and the best time of day and day of week to memorize.

Memora is currently in private beta.
